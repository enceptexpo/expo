About us

Influx Group was started in 2003 in Mumbai (India) with a small budget and a very small team with one common vision to achieve more.

Started with a very Micro plant a decade ago with intentions and will to grow , Influx have now transpired to 25000 sqft plant in palghar, Maharashtra with a thrust to expand to 38000 sqft in coming years. Our manufacturing plant is equipped with GMP, HACCP, ISO 22000, ISO 14000 with well equipped machinery, Quality control department and competent manpower.

We Welcome you all to the world of Health and Wellness & expand your association with us.

When we think of past, I feel ecstatic that within a span of a decade we have travelled many miles and hurdles.

Our aim is to produce innovative range of products with good concept, quality and build good network of customer. We have our large Clients base in PAN INDIA and have presence in 4 continents.

Our Major business is to do Contract manufacturing, Franchisee & Marketing of Nutraceuticals, Ayurvedic and Veterinary Feed Supplements. We can produce economic batch sizes with competitive rates for various categories of products as Soft Gelatin, Tablets, Capsules, Liquid Orals, Oral Powders, Creams, Gels, Ointments, External Oils etc.
